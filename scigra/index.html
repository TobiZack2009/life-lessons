Scientific Benefits of Gratitude
Gratitude is the appreciation for help received from someone. Even though the Bible says we should be grateful, gratitude has been proven time and time again to have scientific benefits. Let's take a look at some of them.
  1. Motivation
    Gratitude helps to motivate people. Its just a natural part of our psychology. If you say thank you to a person, they are likely to help you next time.
  2. Health
    As unrelated as the two subjects may be, gratitude can actually help your health. In a study, people who had positive and grateful thoughts had lower blood pressure than those who did not. This shows the importance of gratitude as a way to help treat terminal illnesses.
  3. Relationships
    Gratitude can help you strengthen your relationship with others. It reduces envy and malice malice and is a building block for long lasting friendships.
